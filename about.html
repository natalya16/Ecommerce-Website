About NAEV

Founded in Florence, Italy, in 1921, NAEV is one of the world’s leading luxury brands.
Following the House’s centenary, NAEV forges ahead continuing to redefine luxury
while celebrating creativity, Italian craftsmanship, and innovation.

NAEV is part of the global luxury group Kering, which manages renowned Houses
in fashion, leather goods, jewelry, and eyewear.
